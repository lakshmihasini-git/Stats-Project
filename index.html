02 — Case Studies

Behind Your Favourite Apps

Click each app tab to see exactly how statistics shapes your experience.

📸
Instagram
📈 Regression
  • Every post gets an "interest score" before you see it — predicting your likelihood to engage
  • Inputs: reels watched to end, posts liked/saved, accounts you interact with, topics you follow
  • Formula: Score = β₀ + β₁(likes) + β₂(watch_time) + ε
  • The model continuously re-trains based on your latest behaviour

💰 Economic Angle

Higher engagement → more ad exposure → more revenue. Regression creates incentives for creators to produce high-performing content — turning math into a creator economy.

Engagement Score vs. Interaction Rate

▶️
YouTube
🏷️ Classification + 📉 Time-Series
  • Classification: every uploaded video is auto-tagged by content, audio & visuals instantly
  • Watch "Home Cooking" → classified as Cooking Enthusiast → precision ads follow
  • Time-Series (ARIMA): tracks when you watch — evenings avg 19 min/day, night binges run longer
  • Exam season = short clips; holiday = long binge sessions. Algorithm adapts in real-time.

💰 Economic Angle

Extra watch time lifts ad revenue by 20–30%. Creators who upload at optimal times (using time-series data) earn 30–50% more per view.

Viewing Time by Hour of Day

🎵
Spotify
🫧 Clustering (K-Means)
  • Groups 600M+ users with similar listening habits — same genres, artists, tempos & moods
  • No pre-set labels: K-Means discovers hidden groups automatically from behaviour
  • Music popular in your cluster gets recommended → Discover Weekly is born
  • Effectively finds your "music twin" and borrows songs from their taste

💰 Economic Angle

Clustering increases listening time → higher subscription & ad revenue. Indie artists gain visibility through clusters, reducing the monopoly of mainstream labels.

User Clusters by Listening Behaviour

🎬
Netflix
🎲 Probability Models
  • Each thumbnail click = binary outcome modelled as X ~ Bernoulli(p)
  • Click probability: P̂ = Clicks ÷ Impressions
  • A/B testing + Thompson Sampling update probabilities in real-time across millions of users
  • You and your friend see different Stranger Things thumbnails — both personalised for max CTR

💰 Economic Angle

Higher CTR → more titles started → reduced churn → retained subscription revenue. Probability models allocate scarce consumer attention efficiently — core microeconomics in action.
